MATHEMATICS

Statistics

Statistics is a branch of mathematics that deals with the collection, analysis, interpretation, presentation, and organization of data. It plays a crucial role in various fields, including scientific research, social sciences, business, finance, engineering, and, of course, computer science. Some of the key concepts in statistics include

Calculus

Calculus is a branch of mathematics that deals with the study of rates of change and accumulation.Engineers use calculus to analyze and design structures, electrical circuits, control systems, and communication systems.

Probability

Probability has numerous applications in computer science and is an essential tool for understanding uncertainty, randomness, and making data-driven decisions. we use Probability in Machine learning,Data Analytics,Cryptography and many more.

Linear Algebra

Linear algebra is essential for computer graphics, machine learning, and optimization. Matrices, vectors, vector spaces, and eigenvalues are concepts used in solving problems related to these fields.
